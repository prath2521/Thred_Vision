About Us

Established in May 2023, THREDVISION is a dynamic fashion enterprise specializing in high-quality garments for both export and domestic markets. With a state-of-the-art facility housing 150 machines and a dedicated workforce of 200 skilled professionals, we manufacture premium casual & formal shirts and kurtas with precision and efficiency.

Led by Mr. Subhash N and Mr. Sandesh N, our mission is to create world-class garments with a commitment to superior quality, competitive pricing, and timely delivery. We proudly supply to Brand Studio LifeStyle Private Limited (domestic sector) and Lindsey Apparels (exports), producing 3.6 lakh garments annually, with plans for expansion. Our association with reputed labs for testing and third-party inspections ensures that every product meets the highest industry standards.
